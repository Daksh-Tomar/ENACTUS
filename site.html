About Enactus NSUT

Enactus NSUT presents a platform where Start-up companies, NGOs and Corporates are invited to come together for bringing change through the positive power of business and human interaction.
Aspire is the annual entrepreneurship convention organised by Enactus NSIT. It is aimed at being a unique platform for young, dynamic and promising entrepreneurs to meet each other and interact with brilliant corporate and business minds.
Constituted to provide a great opportunity for like-minded students and imminent entrepreneurs to catalyse their entrepreneurial temperament, Aspire promises to give an impetus to business intellect, drive the need for lateral thinking, aid in kick-starting profitable startups and realising practical business plans and assist in tapping business potential.
This year, the convention will be replete with an ambitious and enterprising air with events such as Business Quiz, B-plan, Social B-plan, Case Study, Online Elevator Sales Pitch and Online Photography Competition being organised.
It will also witness an engaging Panel Discussion and inspiring Speakers' Session where eminent individuals from all walks of life will share their acumen and experience with the audience.
